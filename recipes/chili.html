Mom's Comfort Chili

A hearty, meaty dish that can warm you from the inside out

Growing up in the cold harsh winters of Wisconsin, only a meal of great strength and warmth could keep one from succumbing to the elements. One Bowl of this delicious chili and you will be ready to weather any storm.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef or Chuck
  • 3 cans of beans (assortment of black and red/kindey beans recommended)
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes in juices
  • 1 pack of chili seasoning

Preparation Steps

  1. Bring a medium or large pot to medium heat on stovetop
  2. Brown the beef or chuck, breaking up the meat into small bite-sized pieces in the process
  3. Once completely browned, drain the meat of any rendered grease
  4. Return pot with meat to stove and add tomatoes with juice, beans, and chili seasoning
  5. Stir to combine all ingredients
  6. Bring mixture to a boil, reduce to a simmer and continue until beans are fully cooked through
