Heartwarming Hot Chocolate

The holiday season never tasted so divine

The only thing missing from your cozy time of snuggling in your PJs by the fireplace is a mug of this delicious beverage. Allow the velvet smooth cocoa to serenade you in soft and sweet embrace.

Ingredients

  • 1 Gallon Whole Milk
  • 1 Pint Heavy Cream
  • 1 can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 2 bags semi-sweet morsels choc chips
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Preparation Steps

  1. Add the chocolate chips and sweetened condensed milk to a microwave safe container
  2. Microwave the mixture in 30 second increments, stiring between, until all chocolate chips are melted. Set aside.
  3. To a slow cooker, add the milk, cream, and cocoa powder. Set heat to 'high' and stir frequently until the mixture starts to simmer
  4. Reduce temperature to low and add melted chocolate chip mixture.
  5. Stir until all combined and serve warm
  6. If desired, garnish with any combination of whipped cream, marshmallows, and cinnamon
